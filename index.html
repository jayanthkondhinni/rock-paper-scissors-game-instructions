Rules for Rock Paper Scissors game build by Jayanth

* User have five chances to play the game.................
* Each time user have to select one of three options[r,p,s].
* computer will randomly select one of [r,p,s] and then well known "ROCK PAPER SCISSORS" rules are applied.
* For every winning trail user will get 10 points as score
* For every losing trail computer will get 10 points as score
* after 5 trails your score gets compared with computer's score and then winner is evaluated accordingly
